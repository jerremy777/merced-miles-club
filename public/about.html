About

Merced Miles Club is centered around running around Lake Merced in San Francisco in the counter-clockwise direction. The route is fixed and does not change.

People participate in different ways. Some run the loop occasionally, while others return to it over longer periods of time. There is no expected pace and no timeline for completion.

The club does not organize group runs or maintain schedules. There are no rankings, and participation is generally individual.

Membership is informal. If you run around Lake Merced counter-clockwise, you are participating.
